Ramen

Ingredients

Ramen, Meat, Vegetables (optional), Eggs (optional)

Ramen: Boil some Water. After Boiled, add in the Ramen. Leave it in for a While.

Meat: Cook the Meat until Ready, and add it to the Ramen. Add any of the optional Ingredients and Enjoy!

Fried Rice

Ingredients

3-4 cloves of garlic (minced), 1 Chili pepper (sliced), 3 Egg yolks, 2 eggs , 6 cups leftover rice, 2 Tbsp MSG (optional), 4 Tbsp butter, 3 Tbsp Soy Sauce, 3 tsp White Pepper, 3-4 Green Onions (sliced), Tahdig, Wok

In a pan on medium high heat, add 1 tbsp butter and toss in â…• of your rice, pressing down and letting it get crispy to make tahdig. In a wok, heat 1 tbsp of pork fat or butter and coat the entirety of the wok. In a bowl, scramble your eggs and yolks and season with 1 tsp of white pepper. Pour into wok and cook on medium heat, mixing constantly to break up into smaller pieces. Add in chili pepper and 2 cloves of minced garlic. Mix and fry until aromatic. Add in the rice and add MSG (optional), soy sauce, green onion, white pepper and your tahdig crispy rice. Mix and fry until combined, plate, and top with more green onion.
