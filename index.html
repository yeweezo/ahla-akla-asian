Burger

Buns: 1 cup + 2 tbsp warm water, 2 tbsp active dry yeast, 1/3 cup vegetable oil, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 egg, 3 cups AP flour, Salt (to taste), Pickles: 1 cup white vinegar, 1 cup water, Salt (to taste), 2 small cucumbers, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, Burger: 16oz beef Burger Sauce: 3 tbsp ketchup, 1/4 cup chopped pickles

To make buns, combine water and yeast and whisk together. Add vegetable oil and sugar, and whisk again. Let this sit for 5 minutes. Add egg, flour, and salt in a mixing bowl and knead about 3-5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Drop dough onto a floured board and cut into six balls. Roll into small balls and sit on a greased tray. Let the dough rest for several minutes and grind the beef. After resting the dough balls for 10 minutes, paint them in egg wash. Bake at 425 Fahrenheit (218 C) for 10 minutes or until golden brown. Brush the buns with egg wash again and add sesame seeds. Place back in the oven until seeds are set. Pickles and sauce: For pickles, add vinegar, water, and salt to a small saucepan and heat until sugar is dissolved. Pour over the sliced cucumber and let sit for 15 minutes. For special sauce, combine mayo, ketchup, pickles, hot sauce, salt, and pepper. Burger: Sear beef in ring molds to get perfect shape, then add cheddar and steam until melted. Slice and toast the buns in a pan. Assemble the burger to your liking and enjoy.

Hot Dogs

Ingredients

1 all-beef hot dog, 1 poppy seed hot dog bun, 1 tablespoon yellow mustard, 1 tablespoon sweet green pickle relish, 1 tablespoon chopped onion, 2 tomato wedges, 1 dill pickle spear, 2 sport peppers, 1 dash celery salt

Bring a pot of water to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low, place hot dog in water, and cook until warmed through, about 5 minutes. Remove hot dog from water and set aside. an overhead view of a single hot dog boiling in a pot of water. Place a steamer basket into the pot and steam poppy seed bun until warm, about 2 minutes. an overhead view of a poppy seed hot dog steaming in a steaming basket. Place hot dog in steamed bun. Pile on toppings in this order: mustard, relish, onion, tomato, pickle, peppers, and celery salt. The tomato wedges should be nestled between hot dog and top of bun on one side. Place pickle between hot dog and bottom of bun on the other side. Don't even think about ketchup!

Buffalo Wings

Ingredients

6 chicken drums, 2 cups pickle juice, 2 cups buttermilk, 1 stick butter, 2 cups ketchup, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar, Squeeze lemon juice, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce, Pinch smoked paprika, Hickory smoke (optional), 2 cups potato starch, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp old bay, 1 tsp lemon pepper, 1 tsp truffle salt

Combine butter, ketchup, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, lemon, honey, Worcestershire, paprika, and optional puff of hickory smoke into pan over medium heat and whisk until well combined. Boil this mixture off until it reaches the correct consistency, then set aside. Add the drums to a bowl and cover in buttermilk and pickle juice. Allow this to brine 30 minutes. Add potato starch, garlic powder, onion powder, old bay, lemon pepper and truffle salt to a large bowl. Dredge your chicken wings in the starch mixture and tap off excess. Add to the frying oil at 325 Fahrenheit for about 6 minutes until just about fully cooked. (NOTE: I said 375 to start the video, which is incorrect.) Set these aside and heat oil to 375 F, then fry again until golden brown and crispy.
